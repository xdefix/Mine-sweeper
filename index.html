Game info
The Minesweeper game begins with a blank square grid. When you click one of the squares, it disappears, while the others remain blank or have numbers on them. You must use the numbers to figure out which empty squares contain mine and which are safe to click on.

Minesweeper can only be played with the mouse. Use the left and right mouse buttons to navigate. Non-mined squares are clicked with the left mouse button, while mined squares are flagged with the right mouse button.

Click "x" or outside the info window to continue to game.
